About Us

ILEE HR SOLUTIONS stands as a premier Training & Placement organization dedicated to bridging the gap between aspiring professionals and the dynamic Hospitality & Retail industries. Promoted by the Divit Foundation, a distinguished not-for-profit organization, we are committed to creating meaningful career pathways through excellence in education and strategic industry partnerships.

Our comprehensive approach combines cutting-edge soft skills development with essential IT competencies, ensuring our candidates are not just job-ready, but career-ready. We take pride in our holistic methodology that transforms raw talent into industry-leading professionals who drive organizational success across hospitality and retail sectors.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with world-class training and create sustainable employment opportunities in the Hospitality & Retail industries through innovative skill development programs, strategic industry partnerships, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred Training & Placement partner in the Hospitality & Retail sectors, recognized for our ability to transform lives, enhance careers, and contribute to industry growth through exceptional human resource development.
